    Xiatian Zhu

Xiatian Zhu is a researcher at Samsung AI Centre, Cambridge, UK, and a Visiting Lecturer at University of Surrey. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Vision from the Queen Mary University of London. He was a postdoctoral researcher at Queen Mary University of London and Vision Semantics Limited. His research interests include computer vision and machine learning.
